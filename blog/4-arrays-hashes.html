Arrays

Arrays organize information in a very effecient way. They're usually used to group together concepts that fit into a family, or overarching concept. They enable the coder to pull from that category or concept any specific item that's needed. In a way, it's an organizational tool.

Hashes

Hashes create what would best be described as "keys." When an item is selected, the outcome it's been assigned to results. Income, outcome. Input, output.
