Arrays

Arrays organize information in a very effecient way. They're usually used to group together concepts that fit into a family, or overarching concept. They enable the coder to pull from that category or concept any specific item that's needed. In a way, it's an organizational tool. It's also used quite extensively with iteration. This is when a particular method needs to be applied to separate elements, one after the other. The method treats each element individually, but carries out the same functions.

Hashes

Hashes organize data into key/value pairs. When an item is selected, the outcome it's been assigned to results. Income, outcome. Input, output. They're useful for translating or encrypting, as well as linking related categories with their respective concepts.
