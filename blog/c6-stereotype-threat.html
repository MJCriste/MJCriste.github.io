Social Identity Threat

I have been impressed with the Dev Bootcamp experience over the last six weeks, and this blog entry prompt adds even more enthusiasm. This topic resonates with me on many levels and I think it's important in order to empower the students to perform closer to their capacity.

I'm so glad that DBC understands the importance of working through these issues, because this work is very collaborative and conducted in a very social manner, so if we don't know who we are, or we don't like ourselves, or we believe that we're inferior because of whatever label we've bought into (right or wrong), we're terribly crippled psychologically and emotionally. We're not mentally healthy and our self-esteem isn't derived from rationality, so then how are we ever going to be able to perform at our capacity? A healthy mind is a productive mind.
